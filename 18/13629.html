  General Notes:

I, Daniel Willets, 309 Chestnut Ave., Trenton, N. J.; born March 21, 1846; married October 6, 1870; hereby apply for membership in The Society of the Sons and Daughters of the Pilgrims by right of descent from Gov. William Coddington, came to Boston in 1630; born 1601 at Lincoln, England; died November 1, 1678. Service: First Governor of Rhode Island.
I was born in New York City.
1. I am the son of Edmund Willets, born April 6, 1800; died March 10, 1875; and his wife Martha Whitson, born February 27, 1807; died September 10, 1883; married October 29, 1829.
2. The said Martha Whitson-Willets was the daughter of Thomas Whitson, born March 4, 1776; died September 1, 1846; and his wife Ann Willets, born August 28, 1779; died October 10, 1869; married March 24, 1804.
3. The said Ann Willets-Whitson was the daughter of Jacob Willets, born 1743, died July 15,1836, and his wife Hannah Powell; married January 1, 1766.
4. The said Jacob Willets was the son of Daniel Willets, and his wife Phebe Carpenter; married December 3, 1742.
5. The said Phebe Carpenter was the daughter of James Carpenter and his wife Mary Willets.
6. The said Mary Willets-Carpenter was the daughter of Andrew Willets, born 1655, died 1712, and his wife Anne Coddington, born 1663, died 1751; married 1682.
7. The said Anne Coddington-Willets was the daughter of Gov William Coddington, born 1601, died 1678, and his wife Anne Brindley, married 1649.
