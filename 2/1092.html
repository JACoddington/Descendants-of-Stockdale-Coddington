  General Notes:

His home was the third place east from the Mt Horeb Methodist Episcopal Church on the Mt Horeb road. The house is a fine old building, still standing and well kept according to Harold J. Coddington at his writing in 1974.
Isaiah was married twice, his wives are buried on each side of him in the Mt Horeb Church Cemetery, but only his second wife has a stone today. He was a toolmaker by trade. 545
