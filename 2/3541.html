  General Notes:

John Yaryan Coddington owned a farm near Love Hill, Indiana. His hobby was fast horses. He owned a speedy stallion. He had a race track on his farm where he trained his colts. For training and developing his colts he had a high two wheel sulky. Linnie, his stepdaughter, took special delight hitching the fast trotting stallion to the sulky and running it around the track urging it to faster speed, laughing gaily with her hair streaming full length behind. 2396
