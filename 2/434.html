  General Notes:

Joseph served in 1779, as a Private in the Middlesex Co., NJ, militia during the Revolutionary War, for which he received £2:16:04 on 8 June 1784 and £2:11:10 on 1 April 1786 (for service in Matthew Freeman's Company.
Gravestone says, "In Memory of James CODDINGTON, Who d. March 21st 1816, in 61st year of his age Afflictions sore short time I bore. Physicians were in vain; Till God above did her my moan And cured my of my pain.".
Reported to have commanded the bodyguard which Washington gave to Lafayette at the Brandywine, and was wounded there (Myers, 1995: 713). 365,612,778,779
