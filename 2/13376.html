  General Notes:

Where is Hiram? Not 1850, 1860, 1870 (the 1890 burned up).
Hiram appears on various censuses in Clinton Co., Indiana--the 1900 gives his birth as Nov. 1831, and he consistently gives Ohio as his birthplace. he had several children and about 25 known descendants through 1900. He doesn't seem to fit in any of the resident Ohio families--almost all the 1831 birth slots are taken by others, although Isaac 1793 and Sarah Hunt Kelsey are marginal possibilities. He died in Union Co., Indiana, where a number of Coddingtons lived, yet few of them had ancestors from or who lived in Ohio. He must be a member of one of those relatively few emigrations that established the Ohio and Indiana branches.
Another line of thought is that he could possibly a relative (brother?) of Martin Luther Coddington (1820-1866), as his son bore that name.
Are two Hiram Coddingtons confused here? Four marriage records in Indiana for Hiram Coddington. 1900 census gives birth as Nov. 1831 (thus 68 years old) but death record gives age as 59.
Familysearch says his father was Samuel Coddington, but no data on Samuel. Which Samuel? 5504
