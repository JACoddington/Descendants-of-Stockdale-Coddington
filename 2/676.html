  General Notes:

Came to Ohio in 1808 via flatboat down the Monongahela with brothers William, Robert, John, and David.

A brick and stone mason, and in the winter boated between Cincinnati and New Orleans. The house which he built and in which he died is still standing in the heart of Cincinnati (1892). (per Rev. Herbert G. Coddington)

From a letter from George Warren Coddington..

"Father and mother with - brothers William, Robert, John, David (or Daniel), my father Stephen Coddington with a lot of other relations, came down the Ohio River on a flat boat and landed at Fort Washington, now Cincinnati, Ohio and were some of the earliest settlers of this state in the Miami Valley."

"One of my cousins James Coddington lived near or at Peoria, Illinois. I believe has a record of my line of family. There was a William Coddington in Cinn., somehow a relative. He had a son, George W. C. who went to Cal. in the fifties or during the gold excitement." 944
