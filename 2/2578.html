  General Notes:

Probably the David Coddington of the story re capture of Tory Col. Christopher Billop.
According to Morris's History, slaves were born to his slaves in 1801 and 104. According to 1820 census, owned one slave.
Although John Insley Coddington identified David c. 1757 as a son of James Coddington 1725, the letter by Moses Coddington, son of David Coddington and Ann Stone, refers to his brother David, living on Staten Island, with "four boys, all living." There was only one David Coddington living on Staten Island at this time and he is reasonably well-documented. 426,2023,2024,2025,2026
