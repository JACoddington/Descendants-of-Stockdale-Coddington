It was during this second enlist that he served with William Fellows at Fort Mason, Arizona.

Codington reenlisted at San Francisco on Dec. 7, 1864, in Co. B, 2nd Calif. Cavalry. He was discharged on Mar 10, 1865, to accept a commission as a 2nd Lieut. in Co. C, Calif. Native Cavalry. Codington was promoted to 1st Lieutenant on July 25, 1865, and transferred to Company D on August 28 to serve as the company's adjutant. He was posted to Fort Mason, Arizona, and later discharged at Drum Barracks, Calif., on March 20, 1866.
