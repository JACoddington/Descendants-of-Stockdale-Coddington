BOSTON BIRTHS, BAPTISMS, MARRIAGES, AND DEATHS, 1630-1699
1656, page 56. John Jephson & Emm Coddington widow were married 7th--3rd mo. by Major Humph. Atharton.
"After the death of her second husband in 1688, Emm became a midwife, and she testified before the Supreme Judicial Court of Massachusetts in a good many cases involving charges of fornication against various couples who had produced untimely offspring."
