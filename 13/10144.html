  General Notes:

Dear Sir,
I was going through my grandparents papers and found a letter to them written by Stella Coddington, wife of Earl R. Coddington. It appears they were friends probably all the way back where Earl and my grand father were born in Missouri. I am attaching the letter for you and also the farm lease they had with my grandparents. You have done so much work on your family history, I thought you might like to have this for your files. On the lease Stella shows her middle initial as M. and you show A. so I do not know which is correct.
My grandparents were George and Eva (Coffin)Thruston. George was born around Humansville, Missouri in January of 1890, moved to Kansas, to Oklahoma, to Colorado to Idaho and finally to California. It looks like the Coddington family also went from Missouri to Kansas, Oklahoma and Idaho. They may have been friends a very long time. George and Eva had two sons and a daughter. This may be interesting, the daughter is named Stella. I have not found any other relationship other than friends for these couples, but you may know of some. George Thruston's parents were William Thruston and Emma Coberly. The Coberly was a large Missouri family.
Congratulations on all the hard work you have done and I hope the attachments are readable.
Sincerely,
Joan Walker
