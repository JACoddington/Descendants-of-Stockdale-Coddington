  General Notes:

Served as a Private in Capt. Richardson's Company (regiment of Col. Lucas) in the Virginia Militia for 6 months in 1780 during Revolutionary War. Pension of $20.00 per annum awarded 12 Feb. 1833.
Pension application gives age as 69 years.
"That on the first of October 1780. Sometime after the battle of Guilford Court House in the County of Hampshire in the State of Virginia, he was drafted to serve a tour of three months duty in the army of the Revolution. in the service of Virginia State Line." Thereafter enlisted for three more months, got sick, and returned home.
His brother Caleb also signed a deposition testifying to Silas' service. 3490
