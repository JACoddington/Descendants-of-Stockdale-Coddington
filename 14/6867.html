  General Notes:

Was engaged to marry, but had to jump out of the school house went it caught on fire and hurt her back. Due to injury broke off her engagement and never married. Was an invalid all her life. A spinster aunt that moved around to live with various family members. Fletcher Ayres made an apartment in the "old house." Ate nothing but bananas.
Was living with Hiram Douthitt in Akron, Ohio to take care of her mother. Margaret Eliza Douthitt had had surgery and was supposedly recovering (probably cancer). Eva was supposed to stay with her, but went out when she should have stayed home, and her mother died while she was out. Rest of family never let her forget it.
Buried in A. T. Ayres mausoleum, Howard, Kansas. 255,3491
