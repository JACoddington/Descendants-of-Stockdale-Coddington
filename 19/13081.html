  General Notes:

Colonel John BUCK was buried in Lexington, Kentucky cemetery, near the Henry Clay statue. Lieutenant Colonel of Virginia Militia; Tax Commissioner and later Sheriff of Frederick County, Va. (formerly Orange County, now Warren). He emigrated to Kentucky along with Marquis Calmes IV, Marquis Richardson, John Richardson, Benjamin Coombs and Samuel Price about 1785-90 and settled in Woodford Country near what is now the town of Versailles. His grave is in the Lexington, KY cemetery, near the Henry Clay statue.
-- charts of H. N. Buck, 1965 5032
