  General Notes:

LEWIS Noe was brother of John, he came up from Woodbridge, and bought the farm formerly owned by Thomas Darling, where Smith M. Miller now lives. He married Phebe Mundy, daughter of Henry Mundy, of Metuchen. He died 5th April, 1838, aged 73 years. She died 11th May, 1814, aged 54 years; they had children: (1st Gen.) 3053
