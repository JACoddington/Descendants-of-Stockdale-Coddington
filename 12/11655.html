  General Notes:

Link not proven between Benjamin (1786-1877) and Joseph (1756-). However, in the 1850 census for Wawarsing, Ulster Co, New York, on p. 43, appear:
633_658. John W Coddington, wife Phebe J., aged 31 and 26 respectively, with children.
634_659. Benjamin and Parthena, aged 55 and 52, with Daniel, 30.
635_660. William Wesley, in whose household resides Samuel Coddington, aged 54
636_661. Judson Coddington, with his family.

John W. was Benjamin's son. Judson's brother was Samuel, and he also had a brother named Benjamin about whom little was known. The name of the fathers of Benjamin, and Judson and Samuel, was Joseph.
Therefore it seems likely that the Benjamin who married Parthena was the same Benjamin, brother to Samuel and Judson, and that therefore the two Joseph's as fathers are also the same person. 233
