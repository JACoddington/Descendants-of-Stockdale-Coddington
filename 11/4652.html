  General Notes:

Ref; Baptized in Etton, Yorkshire, England on 20 Dec 1584;
Graduated from Cambridge University with a B. A. 1605 and a M. A. in 1609.
Ref; Second Wife, Ann sought to be Hammond or a Himmock.
Imprisoned for Independent Worship in 1632, released on ail in 1634. Banished to America upon his release.
Became a Freeman on 7 Jun. 1637 (PCR 1:60)
With 6 of his 7 living children, sons Thomas , Samuel, Joseph and Benjamin and 32 Members of his Church, and many others, sailed on the "Griffin" from London to Boston.
Ref; Mayflower Source Records pg. 519.
1601; Matriculation - Queen's College, Cambridge
