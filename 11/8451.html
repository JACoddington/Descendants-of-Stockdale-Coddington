  General Notes:

Joseph Henry Coddingon and Mary Jentes "had two little girls, and the mother died very young. One of the girls married a Robinson, and their son lives in Oxford, Ohio. I've gotten in touch with them. They know very little about the family history. The little girls lived with Sarah B. Young Corrington and her husband John for several years. Then the girls lived with the mother's family, the Jentes, and upon their death were placed in an orphanage." 2848
