Contractor, Baptist, Republican. Enlisted at Blanchester Ohio 12 Sep 1861, aged 28 as private Co. C, 54th Ohio Infantry. Promoted Corporal 31 Oct. 1861, wounded in action at Shiloh, 6 Apr 1862 and at Pittsburg Landing, Tenn., 7 Apr 1862. Commissioned 2nd Lieutenant 52nd, Colored U.S. Inf. Civil War. Resigned commission 21 Jan 1865. Pension WC683007.
