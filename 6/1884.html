  • Buried: Door Village Cmty, Laporte, LaPorte Co., Indiana, USA

  General Notes:

Elmer's given name was John Ellsworth Coddington, as verified on the 1870 Census. He told his mother there were too many Johns in his school and that he wanted to change his name to Elmer. His mother gave permission and ever since all of his papers have shown Elmer E.
