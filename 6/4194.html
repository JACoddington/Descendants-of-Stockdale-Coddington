Arthur Carl EKLUND 133

  • Born: 21 Mar 1969, San Rafael, Marin Co., California, USA
  • Marriage (1): Rosanne Alicia MORTON on 15 Feb 1997

  General Notes:

Born with pneumonia in one lung, the other lung collapsed with a hole in it. He underwent open chest surgery at 1 1/2 hours old. At 5 days old he contracted spinal meningitis. He recovered from all that and was an excellent student and athlete. He played football and baseball in high school. Golf was his game as an adult, taking after both grandfathers. He graduated from Arizona State University in May 1992 with an Engineering degree with Computer Graphics as his major. After graduation he worked at Goldminer's Daughter Ski Resort in Alta, Utah. After returning to the Phoenix area he studied Hotel Management and was in a management training program with the Ritz Carleton Hotel. He returned to school at ASU and got a second degree in teaching. In 1999 his first teaching assignment was a 5th/6th grade combination, at Arcadia Neighborhood Learning Center in the Scottsdale, AZ, school district. (Mary Kay Coddington Eklund)


Arthur married Rosanne Alicia MORTON on 15 Feb 1997. (Rosanne Alicia MORTON was born on 8 Apr 1972 in , , , Australia.)
