  General Notes:

Notes from WHW's interview with Mabel E. (Alsdorf) Coddington:
"At Accord I was advised to call on Chester. He was at work but his wife, who was just about to start out DEER HUNTING, invited me in and put off her departure until I had copied all the records. A modest little home, not neat. He knows a lot about the Coddingtons, his wife stated. And she brought up the fact that a Coddington "was the first governor of Rhode Island."
