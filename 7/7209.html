  General Notes:

Robert took the family to Michigan (ex Tompkins Co. New York Church Records) about 1837 (HGC Papers). Montgomery Co MI Marriage Records indicate that a Reuben P. married a Harriet Kilbourn there in 1841, and a Martin S. married Eliza K in 1844. These match two of Robert's sons. The incorrect middle initial is probably a mistake. Evidently the Coddington brothers married the Kilbourn sisters.
