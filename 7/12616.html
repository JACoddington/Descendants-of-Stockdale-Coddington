  General Notes:

Robert Catlett was born in 1720/21 at Virginia. He married Mary Ann Floyd circa 1745. Robert Catlett died on 26 February 1803 at Frederick County, Virginia; From the bible of John Catlett, son of Robert, comes the following: "'Robt. Catlett, my father, departed this life Feb. 26, 1803, 2:30 o'clock, age 82 years.'" His estate was probated between 2 January 1804 and 3 February 1806 at Frederick County, Virginia; Robert Catlett seems not to have left a will. His estate is appraised and sales account rendered Jan. 2, 1804. (Stubbs, pp. 51 and 73) Stubbs' citation for the above is Frederick Co. Records, Book 7, p. 191. (Stubbs, p. 52)

From Wills of Frederick County, Virginia, 1795-1816, p. 70 (Frederick Co., VA Will Book 73,4, pp. 189-191): Estate sale: 3 Feb 1806. Includes inventory of property taken by Mrs. Mary Catlett at time of appraisal. Listed among buyers: George, Robert, Mary, David, Jesse and Henry Catlett. He sold land A deed dated 1788 transferred 100 acres from Robert and Mary Catlett to Benjamin Pritchett/Pritchard. The property was located in Prince William Co., VA in the same general area as the property owned by John Catlett, who died in 1778. Verbatim deed to follow.
