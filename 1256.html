  General Notes:

The 1790 census lists a Sarah Corrington with 1 male over 16, one under 16, and three females. Because she is listed as head of household, the male over 16 presumably is not Reuben. The males could be Isaac and John, and two of the three females Sarah and her daughter Sarah, but the third female is unexplained. The 1800 census agains lists Sarah as head of household (over 45), with one male 16-25 and one female 10-15. Her son John married in Nov. 1800, but the male record is probably his. 1380
