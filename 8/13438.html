  General Notes:

David Coddington is not proven as a son of Asher. The assignment is based on David's long residence in Genesee Co., New York, and appearance of William Asher Coddington in Genesee Co. by 1825--after the death of Asher in 1807. Asher and Mary died in November of 1807, and census records indicate that David was born in approximately 1807. Census records say he was born in New York, but presumably that is a mistake. 5518
