  General Notes:

Came to Washtenaw Co, Michigan, then a year later moved to Bath Twp., Clinton Co., Michigan., then returned to Tompkins Co., New York, then returned to Michigan and died at his son's residence in Tecumseh, Lenawee Co., Michigan. Enlisted 4 Aug 1862 in Company G, 109th NY Vol Inf. [Civil War], assigned to Army of the Potomac. Later clerk in Commissary Dept. Commissioned 2nd Lt., then 1st Lt., then Acting Assistant Commissar on staff of General George G. Meade at Patrick Station, then at Humphrey Station, then in charge of commissary trains on South Side Road from Petersburg to Burkesville. Mustered out nr Georgetown, DC in June 1865.
Returned to Tompkins Co. in 1865 and engaged in fire and life insurance business, returned to Michigan in about 1877. Soon after purchased farm in Sec. 28, Tecumseh Twp., Lenawee Co.-- 112 acres very near Tecumseh. 4654
