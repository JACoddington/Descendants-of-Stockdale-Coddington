  General Notes:

Samuel Moore was the first town clerk of Woodbridge, New Jersey and occupied the office from 1668-1688.
Moved to Woodbridge from Newbury, Massachusetts with "five daughters, three sons, and two slaves, each 60 acres." (Myers, 1995:650) 73,4946
