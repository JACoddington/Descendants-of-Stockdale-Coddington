  General Notes:

The linkage of Joseph Coddington to Benjamin (1798-aft 1875) is by no means certain. Merlyn Coddington gave his parents as "Benjamin, est. 1800" and "Mary" but gave no argument or evidence for the existence of either, or the relationship to Joseph. The linkage here is based only on the marriage of Benjamin and Janneke in Ellenville, New York in 1825, and Joseph's birth in the same town in 1827.Ellenvile and Marbletown are about 19 miles apart.

His obituary stated:
"JOSEPH CODDINGTON died suddenly from stroke of apoplexy at his home, south side, in Coudersport, Friday, Feb. 21, 1896, age 69. Born and brought up in Ulster county, NY. Came to Coudersport in 1869, and worked at his trade as a stone mason. In 1872 married Theresa Fisher, nee Fourness. Three children. Quiet and unobtrusive in manner, Mr. C. was a good citizen, kind husband, and indulgent father." 2450,2451
