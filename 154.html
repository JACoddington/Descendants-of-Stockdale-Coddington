  General Notes:

John owned some land in Woodbridge which had formerly been the property of James Clarkson. John was a Constable of Woodbridge in 1739. A yeoman.
Excerpt from Will: "Moreover it is my will that the one half of that piece of Salt Meadow in Sunken Marsh which I bought of Samuel Fitzrandolph shall be sold by my Executors hereafter named & my Negro wench calld Bess [evidently a slave] shall in like manner be sold & Applyed towards paying my Just Debts." 454
