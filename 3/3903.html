Frank Lester CODDINGTON 133,2005

  • Born: 8 Feb 1907, Bound Brook, Somerset Co., New Jersey, USA 2005
  • Died: 14 Apr 1995, Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey, USA at age 88 2005

  General Notes:

He lived all his life on his family farm which was acquired by his great-grandfather in 1717. He was a farmer. He also taught all grades at the old Mount Bethel School in Warren Township during the early 1930's. He was an active member of the Mt Horeb United Methodist Church, where he served on the Board of Trustees. His great grandparents were founders of the historic church. (From The History of Mt Horeb United Methodist Church, by Susie Boyce.)
