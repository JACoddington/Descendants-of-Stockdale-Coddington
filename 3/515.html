  General Notes:

Franklin changed their family name to Codington - with one D. He had told Lora that this was the 'old fashioned' way the name had been spelled. Franklin was a farmer, and his son & daughter (Lester and Lora) continued to live on their farm until he died (Lora is still alive in June 2000 and still lives on that farm). Lester went to college and became a teacher, still farmed on the side, and later switched from dairy to fruits (mostly peaches). Franklin had bought farms or houses in other areas (NY state for example) and rented out these other properties.
