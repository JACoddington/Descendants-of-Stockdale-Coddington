  General Notes:

Hotel keeper. First Sheriff in Garrett Co, elected in 1873, served at least through 1878. Buried in Catholic section of cemetery.
According to his obituary, Thomas moved to Wisconsin in about 1853 and there married Ellen Thompson, with whom he had one son, William H. The 1860 census records the family, as well as, presumably, Ellen's mother, Ann Thompson, aged 58. When Ellen died, Thomas moved back to Garrett Co., Maryland, and married his second wife, Cecilia Jamison. 1358,1415,1703
