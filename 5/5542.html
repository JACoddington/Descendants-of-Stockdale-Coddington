  General Notes:

He was a carpenter by trade, and lived at Elizabeth, NJ, and Theatford, MI. He enlisted in Doubleday's 4th New York Artillery in 1862 as a private, and was promoted to second lieutenant, a rank he held at time of discharge from service. [Civil War]
