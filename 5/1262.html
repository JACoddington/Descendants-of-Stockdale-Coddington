  General Notes:

Born at Provost, Now Franklin St, NYC. He learned the carpenter's trade, and afterwards became a grocer in Greenwich St, NYC, but returned to his trade about the time of his marriage. He and his wife were members of the M. E. church. In 1848, having acquired a competency, he moved to Yonkers, where he died. (From Rev. Herbert G. Coddington, D.D.) 57
