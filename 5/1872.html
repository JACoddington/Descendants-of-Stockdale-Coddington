  • Died: 13 Mar 1914, Lebanon, Boone Co., Indiana, USA at age 68

  General Notes:

Enlisted in the one hundred day service with the Ohio Militia [Civil War], and was detailed to hospital service in Virginia where he nursed soldiers ill with measles, until the close of the war. He went into the grocery business in Morrow, OH. In 1880 he moved to Piatt County, IL, where he conducted a grain buying business with his grocery business, and later engaged in the hardware business. (per Gale Schalliol, 11/30/00).
