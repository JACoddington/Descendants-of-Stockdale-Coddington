  General Notes:

A stonecutter while in New York. While he lived in Arkansas, he was a carpenter. There is no death certificate on file in the Arkansas Bureau of Vital Statistics because the people in those days in Desha County 'took care of their own'. Tamar died of typhoid and was buried the same day she died. Joseph and Tamar moved to Arkansas in 1911 or 1912. A little later they moved to Drew County but stayed there only a short time before returning to Halley, this time to live in Halley itself. 156
