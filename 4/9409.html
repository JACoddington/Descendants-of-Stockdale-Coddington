Sarah Ann CODDINGTON 33,1232

  • Born: 14 Sep 1809, New York, New York Co., New York, USA 33
  • Died: 28 Jun 1871, Newark, Essex Co., New Jersey, USA at age 61 33

  General Notes:

Born at 18 Jay St. In early 1871 was quite sick and had a very painful arm that prevented her writing, according to a letter to her from her brother in California, William. He mentioned that her sister Lydia was by her side and that her sister Eliza planned to "go down" and live with her.
In Sept. 1868 she still suffered from "her old complaint in her side." Her sister Lydia was with her and she was enjoying her "new home" in Newark, New Jersey. William had given her five hundred dollars, and wanted her always to write him if in need. 1982,4658
