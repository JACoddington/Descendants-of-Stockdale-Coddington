  General Notes:

James was a miner and worked in a gold mine owned by his uncle William (1820-) in Angels Camp, CA. May 31, 1867, he registered to vote in Angels Camp, CA. On Oct 8, 1868 he left Angels Camp and supposedly returned home following the death of his father.

POSSIBLY James S. Coddington, enrolled at San Francisco, 10 Jan. 1865 as Private, Co. C, 8th California Infantry. Mustered out at San Francisco 24 Oct 1865. Applied for a pension 18 Aug. 1892 aged 50, residence Eagleville, Modoc Co. California. Res. at Likely, CA 18 may 1904. Pension not granted because never completed. [Civil War]. 1276
